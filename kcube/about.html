All About Us and More

The Start

K-CUBE intiative is an educational charity foundation established in 2013.

The purpose of the K-CUBE Scholarship Award is to nurture leadership and entrepreneurial acumen in our beneficiaries. Our aim is that over time our beneficiaries would become change agents in both their local and larger global communities.

Our Aim

To provide professional assistance to associations, corporations, foundations, and individuals in the delivery of financial aid to qualified and deserving students while providing leadership, mentorship, and internship opportunities to our beneficiary students.

Our Goal

  • To offer scholarship opportunities to high performing students at the tertiary level of education
  • To encourage academic excellence, research and nurture leadership and entrepreneurship for our beneficiaries.
  • To be the number one scholarship management service provider in Africa.
  • We hope, in the long term, to become the preferred leadership, management training, and educational research institution on the continent.

Our Goal

  • To raise requisite funds to provide financial support to deserving students till they complete their first degree.
  • To provide mentorship and Leadership training for our scholarship beneficiaries.
  • To provide internship and Job Placement opportunities for our scholarship beneficiaries.
  • To provide Personal Development Training programmes for students at the tertiary education level.
  • To design programs to foster Student Volunteerism
  • To provide professional Scholarship Management Services to both individuals and institutions

Key Support Areas

  • Tuition
  • Accommodation
  • Text Books and Stipend
