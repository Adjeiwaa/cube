Awards

From free mentorship to industrial placement, corporate scholarship grants, free training in leadership and entrepreneurship, career guidance and counselling, skills development, etc., the award is a comprehensive package for undergraduate students. Applicants must have completed at least the first year of any tertiary education programme.

Applications not meeting the above award criteria would be considered on their own merit.

Eligibility

The selection criteria for scholarship beneficiaries are the specific standards used to identify qualified applicants who ultimately will be selected as scholarship recipients.

Prospective beneficiaries should meet at least the following requirements before applying:

  • Must have obtained a senior secondary certificate from WAEC or equivalent
  • Must not have obtained a C in any of their courses taken.
  • Must be on an undergraduate programme in an Accredited University in Ghana
  • Must have completed at least one year of a programme at an accredited tertiary institution in Ghana
  • Must show evidence of high academic performance on their current course of study with a second class upper or better.
  • Must demonstrate financial need.
  • Applicants must disclose if they are beneficiaries of any other form of Scholarship or financial support
  • Must demonstrate willingness to be involved in volunteering work and activities that K- Cube would engage in as part of its programmes.

Selection Criteria

The selection criteria for scholarship beneficiaries are the specific standards used to identify qualified applicants who ultimately will be selected as scholarship recipients.

Primary Criteria

  • Financial need
  • Community/extracuricular involvement
  • Leadership traits
  • Participation in specific activities
  • Awards and recognition
  • Personal attribute
  • Personal statement (400 to 600 words)

Secondary Criteria

  • Demonstrated or expressed interest in leadership and entrepreneurship training
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Academic standing (minimum required cumulative GPA of 3.50)

Download the Application Form Here if you meet the Requirements. Also, see processes for other requirements.

Requirements

To be considered for a K-CUBE Scholarship Award, applicants must complete an application form and return it to K-CUBE before the beginning of the academic year. The application must include:

  1. A completed application forms (Only completed application forms will be considered.)
  2. Two letters of recommendation with at least one from a teacher or school official at the tertiary level.
  3. An official college transcript including GP A.
  4. A Personal statement (400 to 600 words) stating the applicant's qualifications and educational and career goals. Must state why you should be considered for the award.

All four of the above items must be submitted before the application will be considered.

Pledge

Selection would be done in a non-partial and an objective basis

Renewal

Beneficiaries must renew application each year. This is necessary because where a beneficiary’s academic performance falls below a set mark, he or she may be withdrawn from the scheme. Also if financial circumstances change a beneficiary ought to notify us for a review of the level of support to be offered.
